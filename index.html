ABOUT ME

#I love Programming {aka: Googling;}

I may be considered a somewhat unusual case. I started off my university career doing American History. I loved reading books. I figured, hey books = historian? Not so right. Although I much love the history I've learned, it lead me down a path of studying historical natural disasters which eventually transformed me into a geologist. This is what got me into programming and developing. I became fascinated with creating tsunami models based on earthquake faults in different regions of the Earth (I used clawpack if anyone is curious). I wanted to understand how these models functioned and I started reading all the scripts and lines of code behind the scenes. I got them to overlay on Google Earth and then I started wondering...how can I make user friendly interfaces...like websites that allowed people to use super complicated models in a super non-complicated way? It took some time, but finally I'm finishing my certificate in full stack development at the University of Arizona! Now, here I am today. I'm an aspiring programmer, a wanna be cheff and blogger and an Engineer.
